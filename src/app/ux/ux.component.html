UX & UI Design

In this track, participants learn about User Experience (UX) and User Interface (UI) Design, as part of User Centered Design. The modern software design focus on the user, as well as the iterative cycle of "design, review & revise",  is reviewed. The three basic criteria of usability will be discussed.

Participants will learn to about software design within the context of a standard product development process and its phases:

  • Concept Development
  • Design & Prototype
  • Production
  • Post Production & Follow Up

Participants will learn how to create storyboards, flow charts,  wireframes, mockups, personas and user journeys. Industry standard tools, such as Invision, Adobe XD, Balsamiq, and Adobe Photoshop will be reviewed. Participants will go through the design process  and create the deliverables needed for their own course project.
