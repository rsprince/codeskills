About

Code Skills Academy™ has developed a variety of curricula based on current best practices at major corporations in the United States, utilizing industry-standard tools. The courses are based on our professional experiences as development leads, full stack developers, UX designers. We have served as curriculum developers, instructors and facilitators for professional courses delivered at and for Accenture, Macromedia and other organizations.

Richard Sprince has been in the software development and training business for 25+ years within the corporate world. As a curriculum developer and training instructor, he has developed and presented hands-on curriculum and courses, both in-person and online, for Accenture, American Express, The Pratt Institute, New York University, Macromedia and Florida Atlantic University.

In Miami, he has developed and delivered hybrid curriculum to the local community, including computer skills training for Miami Dade Public Schools  and Jobs For Miami, and has served as a volunteer instructor in the K-5 setting.
