Providing Programs for Education

Code Skills Academy™ has developed comprehensive curriculum based on current best practices at major corporations in the United States, utilizing industry-standard tools. Our courses are based on our professional experience as development leads, full stack developers, UX designers. We have served as curriculum developers, instructors and facilitators for professional courses delivered at and for Accenture, Macromedia and other organizations.

Code Skills Academy™ partners with professional development, continuing education, and enrichment programs, universities, colleges and schools to offer their students courses that provide the foundational skill sets needed to develop a career in technology.

Creative Digital Media, Inc. has been providing software development for over 25 years to the corporate world, and has developed and presented hands-on curriculum and courses, both in-person and online, for Accenture, American Express, The Pratt Institute, New York University, Macromedia and Florida Atlantic University.

In Miami, the company has developed and delivered hybrid curriculum to the local community, including computer skills training for Miami Dade Public Schools  and Jobs For Miami.
