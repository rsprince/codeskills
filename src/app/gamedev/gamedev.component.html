Game Development

Game Writing, Coding & Marketing with Roblox

The curriculum focuses on development for the popular gaming platform, Roblox, used worldwide and a favorite of millions. This course incorporates creative writing, game coding with the Lua scripting language, as well as project management, marketing, and collaboration. The application development process is discussed and implemented, utilizing concept writing, storyboarding, wireframing and mockups. Participants work together as a team to develop their own games while building tangible skills.

Benefits

Participants will learn:

  • Project Planning, Organizational Thinking
  • Creative Writing and Storytelling
  • Coding & Problem Solving
  • Teamwork & Collaboration

Course Outline

  • Creative Writing
    How to come up with an idea for a game and develop it.
  • The Development Process
    The application development process is discussed, utilizing concept writing, storyboarding, wire framing and mockups.
  • Game Coding with Lua
    Learn coding to drop and move objects in your game, and allow interaction with players.
  • Project Management Skills
    We discuss where to start, how to keep track of your ideas, and what to do next, as well as tools that will help you do it.
  • Revenue Opportunities
    We will discuss options for generating revenue inside the Roblox environment as well as with ancillary media and products.
  • Marketing
    Once your game is ready (maybe a little before), you need to let the world know it. Options available on roblox.com as well as other social media channels will be discussed.
  • Team Work and Collaboration
    Game coding is a form of software development, which requires multiple skill sets. Working with a team requires different roles and responsibilities. Teams must learn to work together. Participants work together as a team to develop their own games while building tangible skills.
